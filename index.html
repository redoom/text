Tessa Virtue and Scott Moir

Tessa Virtue and Scott Moir are Canadian ice dancers who have left an indelible mark on the world of figure skating. They are renowned for their exceptional skill, artistry, and captivating performances.

Tessa Virtue

Tessa Virtue is a talented ice dancer born on May 17, 1989, in London, Ontario, Canada. She has achieved numerous accolades throughout her career, showcasing elegance and grace on the ice.

Notable Achievements

  • Olympic Gold Medalist (2010, 2018)
  • Multiple World Champion
  • Canadian National Champion

Scott Moir

Scott Moir, born on September 2, 1987, in London, Ontario, Canada, is a highly accomplished ice dancer. His partnership with Tessa Virtue has resulted in some of the most memorable performances in the history of the sport.

Notable Achievements

  • Olympic Gold Medalist (2010, 2018)
  • Multiple World Champion
  • Canadian National Champion

Legacy in Ice Dance

Tessa Virtue and Scott Moir's legacy in ice dance extends beyond their impressive medal collection. Their innovative routines and emotional connection on the ice have inspired a new generation of figure skaters.

Retirement

In 2019, Tessa Virtue and Scott Moir officially announced their retirement from competitive figure skating. They continue to contribute to the sport through mentorship and choreography, leaving an enduring impact on the world of figure skating.
